LAMENTS OF THE FALLEN

Coming Soon

A journey through ten tracks of pure gothic doom metal, wrapped in dark and melancholic atmospheres. Deep growls, intense screams, ethereal female vocals, and evocative lyrics bring to life a powerful and haunting sound.

The essence of gothic and doom merges in an album that tells a tale of torment, damnation, and eternal perdition.

From the shadows of Tears of Darkness emerges Klod’s solo journey: a path through sorrow and damnation, where his growls intertwine with Luana’s ethereal voice in this first gothic doom metal album.

Future works will venture into darker realms — gothic black and gothic death metal — expanding the use of scream vocals and pushing further into sonic darkness.
