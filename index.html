About the project

Necrovia

Necrovia is a gothic doom metal duo formed by Klod Cripta and Luana. Born from previous solo work and years of shared musical ground, the project now stands as a single identity built on contrast: guttural darkness, ethereal vocals and mournful atmospheres.

The music is created independently, with modern tools, home recording and direct artistic control. No commercial ambition is required: only the need to give form to darkness.

Behind the Music

High-quality
home recording

Necrovia operates through a high-quality home recording process. Every track is built, shaped and completed in a fully controlled environment, where composition, vocals and post-production remain tightly connected.

Vocals are recorded with dedicated gear and mixed in REAPER on Arch Linux. This approach keeps the sound direct, raw and intentional — without external interference.

Studio setup

  • Arch Linux · KDE Plasma
  • REAPER DAW
  • Audient iD4 MKII
  • RØDE NT1 condenser mic
  • Audio-Technica ATH-M20x
