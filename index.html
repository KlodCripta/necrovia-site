About the project

Necrovia

Necrovia is a gothic doom metal duo formed by Klod Cripta and Luana. Born from previous solo work and years of shared musical ground, the project now stands as a single identity built on contrast: guttural darkness, ethereal vocals and mournful atmospheres.

The music is created independently, with modern tools, home recording and direct artistic control. No commercial ambition is required: only the need to give form to darkness.

Klod Cripta

Growl, scream, lyrics, vocal lines and artistic direction.

Luana

Clean vocals, ethereal harmonies and female vocal lines.

Behind the Music

High-quality
home recording

Necrovia works through a high-quality home recording method: instrumental foundations are shaped, edited and completed with original lyrics, vocal lines and post-production inside a Linux-based setup.

Vocals are recorded with dedicated gear and mixed in REAPER on Arch Linux, keeping the process independent, controlled and close to the emotional core of each song.

Studio setup

  • Arch Linux · KDE Plasma
  • REAPER DAW
  • Audient iD4 MKII
  • RØDE NT1 condenser mic
  • Audio-Technica ATH-M20x
